• Camp Gladiator

    Trainer's iPad App

  • Since 2008, CG has been the fastest growing group fitness company in the US. By uniquely combining fitness and technology CG has enabled trainers to postively impact the lives of millions of people across the US.

    This project is the complete redesign of the Trainer's iPad App used to connect trainers and campers during the group workouts at camp.

    The app alows the trainers to have a full overview of their campers and manage them. It also works as a tool for sales as it possible to sell and upgrade memberships. Another main feature is the ability to check-in campers to the training sessions.
