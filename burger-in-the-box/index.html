• Burger In The Box

    Branding & UI

  • Burger in the box is a online shop to order fronzen gurmet burgers. The goal is to make people feel like chefs. There are five different burgers to let your creativity grown. You can share the recipes you create and get recipes from other burger lovers. The idea came from two friends of mine involved in the food industry and they gave me the opportunity to design their brand identity and desktop/mobile experiences.
