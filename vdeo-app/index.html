• Vdeo App

    On-Demand Mobile Video Streaming Platform

  • Vdeo is a product for a mobile video streaming platform for all Latin America, for iOS and Android, with short series and news documentaries. The users can watch up to three free videos per week or subscribe to get access to the entire catalog.

    I worked together with a team of business analysts and content strategists between Brazil, Argentina, and Mexico to create the best UX for its business model. I was responsible for the UX and UI, from Information Architecture/Interaction Design to the Visual Design. The colors, typeface, graphic elements and overall look & feel were chosen to represent people from their 20's to mid 30's who focus on news and entertainment.
