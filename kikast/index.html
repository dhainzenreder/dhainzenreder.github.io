• Kikast

    Sports App

  • Kikast is originally a website launched on November 1st, 2013. The app allows anyone to commentate a sports game in live audio, as if he was a radio sports commentator. He is broadcasted live on the app on which anyone can participate, by recording short audio messages, 15 seconds max, that are then broadcasted live to the community.

    Thanks to the brothers Ness Benamran and David Benamran, founders of Kikast, for the opportunity to design their iPhone and Android apps.
