• Eygl

    Branding & Android App

  • Eygl is an Android app of a French startup that help friends to stay connected with each other. It is a new concept of address book based on the frequency users connect with their friends, sugesting those to keep in touch.

    My job was to creat the Logo and Visual Identity for the brand and also the Visual Design of the app based on high delity wireframes I received.

    The Eygl brand communicates values such as Link, Relationship, Proximity, Nodes and Simplicity. The primary color is Purple. From a color perspective, purple combines social, trust, loalty and friendship. While white brings simplicity, the dark purple gives balance with and elegant touch.
