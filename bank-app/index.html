• Bank App

    UI Concept

  • Concept design for a pitch of a new and increasingly popular bank, which wants to take their digital products into the modern age, focusing on making it even easier for their clients to make transactions.

    The app is a modern, fresh banking experience, aimed at early thirties tech consumers with a seamless cross-platform experience that helps their clients feel as secure on mobile as they do on desktop.
