• Crowd stuffer

    Branding, Web Responsive & Apps

  • Crowdstuffer is the ultimate buyer’s guide for crowd funded products, it’s a startup based in the US. Crowdstuffer reviews the hottest products on popular crowdfunding networks like Kickstarter, Indiegogo to name a few, review the product, and provide stats those show the progression of the product from concept to funding.

    The scope of the project was from logo/brand design, visual identity to responsive web site and mobile native app for iOS, Android and Windows Phone.

    The Crowdstuffer brand should communicates values such as Innovative, Simplicity, Informative, Unique, Community, Memorable & Different, User Focus, Fun & Humor. The primary color is Orange. For some reason orange is ideal because it presents a fresh color for the platform. From a color perspective re ects excitement, and enthusiasm. In marketing it creates a call to action, to buy, sell, and subscribe which would t the purpose of the website.
