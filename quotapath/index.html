• QuotaPath

    Commission & Attainment Tracking Web Application

  • Incentive compensation management designed to motivate revenue teams by providing a simple and easy to use commission and attainment tracking application.

    Built for sales professionals looking for a straightforward and easy view into their attainment, compensation, and potential future earnings. From each level (Rep, Manager, Executive), it should answer the following questions with minimal effort - What is my quota? What credit have I received so far this time period? What have I earned this time period? What could I potentially earn this time period? What could I earn if I subtract out taxes?
